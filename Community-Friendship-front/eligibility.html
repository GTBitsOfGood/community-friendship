Eligibility

Community Friendship, Inc. (CFI) is a comprehensive provider of recovery based services assisting adults in metropolitan Atlanta. Our mission is to provide a supportive community for people whose mental illness prevents them from participating in community life, employment and relationships. Our staff are professionally trained and widely recognized in the provision of community-based mental health services. The collective experience of CFI's leadership team spans the public and private sectors, and is the inspiration behind the implementation of our compassionate and specialized supportive services.

Who is eligible for CFI services?

  • Adults ages 18 and up
  • Diagnosed with mental illness or a dual diagnosis
  • Impairment in life activities
  • Individuals meet at least one of the following criteria:
    • Have difficulty with interpersonal relationships
    • Have difficulty with interpersonal relationships
    • Unable to recognize personal danger or inappropriate social behavior
    • Requires help with basic living skills to ensure safety.
    • Repeated interventions by the mental health, social service, or judicial systems are necessary.
