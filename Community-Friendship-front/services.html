SERVICES

As a leading pioneer of psychosocial rehabilitative services, Community Friendship offers a comprehensive range of recovery-based programs. We provide support and training in self-sufficiency and independent living, career development, and full integration in the community. We take a holistic approach, working with the whole person - mind, body, and spirit â€“ and not just their illness. We believe that all individuals have the ability to grow and reach their full potential.

Day Program

We help consumers to develop vocational, social and living skills Read More

Case Management

We help consumers negotiate and obtain needed community services to ensure successful community living.

Work Opportunities

We assist consumers in choosing, getting and keeping real jobs in the community.

Training

We offer a variety of training and recovery education to consumers, families, staff and the community.

Peer Support

We provide support for consumers through the dedication of Certified Peer Specialists (CPS). CPS professionals share their lived experiences to lend unique insight into mental illness and what makes recovery possible.

Residential Services

We offer a wide array of supported housing programs including group homes, supervised apartments, independent apartments, and specialized housing programs for individuals who have been homeless and have a mental illness.

Outreach Services

We provide outreach services to homeless men and women with mental illnesses.

Vocational Rehabilitation Services

We provide vocational rehabilitation including job readiness, job coaching, and supported employment.
